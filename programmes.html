PROGRAMMES OFFERED


Explore our diverse range of academic programs designed to equip you with the knowledge and skills necessary for a successful career in public health. At the Pan African School of Public Health and Tropical Medicine

At the Pan African School of Public Health and Tropical Medicine, we offer a range of advanced programs designed to advance your expertise and career in public health:

  • Master of Science (MSc): Our MSc program provides in-depth knowledge and practical skills in various areas of public health, preparing you for leadership roles in the field.
  • Postgraduate Diploma (PGD): The PGD program offers specialized training and knowledge for those seeking to enhance their qualifications and career prospects in public health.
  • Doctor of Philosophy (PhD): Our PhD program is designed for those pursuing advanced research and academic careers, focusing on significant public health challenges and innovations.
    • Each program is tailored to meet the needs of professionals and researchers, providing a robust foundation in public health and opportunities for meaningful contributions to the field.
